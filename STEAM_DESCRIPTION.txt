[h1]EfD Enhanced - 游戏体验增强模组[/h1]
为 Escape from Duckov 提供局内任务追踪、移动增强、传送前装备检查等功能。
[h2]功能列表[/h2]
[h3]🎯 局内任务追踪[/h3]
在任务详情页面勾选"局内追踪"，进入突袭后在屏幕左上角显示任务进度。
[list]
[*]显示任务完成进度（如 2/3）和已完成子任务（✓）
[*]只显示当前地图的任务
[*]可调整位置、大小、是否显示描述
[*]打开菜单时自动隐藏
[/list]
[h3]🏃 移动增强[/h3]
调整角色移动响应速度。4档位可调：禁用/轻度/中度/重度。
[i]注：调整的是加速度参数，不是移动速度上限。[/i]
[list]
[*]仅影响玩家角色
[*]实时切换，无需重启
[/list]
[h3]🎡 物品轮盘菜单[/h3]
快速访问物品栏的径向菜单。
[b]使用方法：[/b]按住热键（默认 ~）→ 鼠标选择 → 释放使用
[list]
[*]单手操作，无需离开游戏视角
[*]菜单打开时自动屏蔽射击和视角旋转
[*]显示当前物品栏绑定的物品图标
[*]支持自定义热键和缩放（0.5-2.0倍）
[/list]
[h3]💣 投掷物轮盘菜单[/h3]
专门用于快速切换投掷物的径向菜单。
[b]使用方法：[/b]按住 G 键（可配置）→ 鼠标选择 → 释放装备
[list]
[*]自动扫描背包中所有投掷物（手雷、闪光弹、烟雾弹等）
[*]相同类型投掷物自动堆叠显示总数量
[*]菜单打开时自动屏蔽射击和视角旋转
[*]支持自定义热键和缩放
[/list]
[h3]🔫 武器对比[/h3]
在库存中选中一个武器后，悬停到其他武器会显示属性对比。
[list]
[*]支持枪支和近战武器
[*]绿色/红色标识优劣
[*]显示格式：[选中武器值] → [悬停武器值]
[/list]
[h3]✅ 传送前装备检查[/h3]
进入地图前检查并提醒缺少的装备：
[list]
[*]武器和弹药（检查每把枪是否有至少一个弹匣的弹药）
[*]医疗用品、食物、水
[*]天气状况（风暴警告）
[*]任务所需物品（按地图过滤）
[/list]
[h3]⚙️ 设置面板[/h3]
在游戏设置中添加了 EfD Enhanced 选项卡：
[list]
[*]传送前检查项目单独开关
[*]移动增强档位调节
[*]任务追踪器位置、大小、显示选项
[*]界面增强功能开关
[*]物品轮盘热键和缩放
[*]按键绑定（支持键盘、鼠标、手柄）
[*]一键重置
[/list]
[h2]多语言支持[/h2]
完整支持：简体中文、繁体中文、English、日本語

[h1]EfD Enhanced - Gameplay Enhancement Mod[/h1]
Provides in-raid quest tracking, movement enhancement, pre-raid equipment check, and more for Escape from Duckov.
[h2]Features[/h2]
[h3]🎯 In-Raid Quest Tracking[/h3]
Check "In-Raid Tracking" on quest details to display progress on screen during raids.
[list]
[*]Shows completion progress (e.g., 2/3) and completed sub-tasks (✓)
[*]Only shows quests for current map
[*]Adjustable position, scale, and description display
[*]Auto-hides when opening menus
[/list]
[h3]🏃 Movement Enhancement[/h3]
Adjusts character movement response. 4 levels: Disabled/Light/Medium/Heavy.
[i]Note: Adjusts acceleration parameters, not max movement speed.[/i]
[list]
[*]Only affects player character
[*]Real-time switching, no restart required
[/list]
[h3]🎡 Item Wheel Menu[/h3]
Radial menu for quick item bar access.
[b]Usage:[/b] Hold hotkey (default ~) → Move mouse → Release to use
[list]
[*]One-handed operation without leaving game view
[*]Auto-blocks shooting and camera rotation while open
[*]Shows actual item bar icons
[*]Customizable hotkey and scale (0.5-2.0x)
[/list]
[h3]💣 Throwable Wheel Menu[/h3]
Dedicated radial menu for quick throwable selection.
[b]Usage:[/b] Hold G key (customizable) → Move mouse → Release to equip
[list]
[*]Auto-scans all throwables in inventory (grenades, flashbangs, smoke, etc.)
[*]Same-type throwables auto-stack showing total count
[*]Auto-blocks shooting and camera rotation while open
[*]Customizable hotkey and scale
[/list]
[h3]🔫 Weapon Comparison[/h3]
Select a weapon in inventory, then hover over others to see stat comparison.
[list]
[*]Supports guns and melee weapons
[*]Green/red indicators for better/worse stats
[*]Format: [Selected Value] → [Hovered Value]
[/list]
[h3]✅ Pre-Raid Loadout Check[/h3]
Checks and reminds before entering map:
[list]
[*]Weapons and ammo (checks if each gun has at least one magazine worth)
[*]Medical supplies, food, water
[*]Weather conditions (storm warning)
[*]Required quest items (filtered by map)
[/list]
[h3]⚙️ Settings Panel[/h3]
Adds EfD Enhanced tab to game settings:
[list]
[*]Individual toggles for pre-raid checks
[*]Movement enhancement level adjustment
[*]Quest tracker position, size, display options
[*]UI enhancement toggles
[*]Item wheel hotkey and scale
[*]Keybinding (supports keyboard, mouse, gamepad)
[*]One-click reset
[/list]
[h2]Multi-Language Support[/h2]
Fully supports: Simplified Chinese, Traditional Chinese, English, and Japanese
